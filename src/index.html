Field manual

Take command

Computer

Arrow keys move. Z confirms, X cancels, Enter starts, and V selects. Q / E are the shoulder buttons.

Phone & tablet

Tap Deploy once, then use the on-screen GBA controls. Landscape gives the map and controls the most room.

Controller

Any standard gamepad should work immediately. Remap it from the emulator menu if your buttons feel reversed.

Save safely

The original cartridge save works automatically. The menu also offers save states plus save-file import and export.
